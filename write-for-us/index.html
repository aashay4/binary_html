Write for Us!

We welcome individual bloggers and writers to "write for us"! If writing is your passion and you can write creative content then do not hesitate to pitch us. We are accepting articles on Technology, Computer networking, LAN Tricks, and Programming. Before pitching us please go through the guidelines.

Guidelines

  • Your article should be 100% unique, it should not be published anywhere(even on your website)
  • An article should be at least 1000 Words long
  • Do not write for length or just a link back to your website
  • Include real-time examples, research or case study along with the screenshots

What you will get?

You will get two Do-follow backlinks for your website, one in the content and another in the about author.

About us!

Text to Binary is an online tool which provides various technology, LAN, and mathematical solution totally for free. It is powered by Lantech Infocom, a computer networking company.

What's the process?

Please do not start writing an article without our approval. Please share your idea along with the summary of your article at texttobinary4@gmail.com with the subject of "Guest post on Binary to Text". Once we approve your idea, then you can start writing the content.
