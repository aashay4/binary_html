How to convert Decimal to Fraction?

Converting the decimal values to fractions is quite simple. Let's understand it with an example. For example, if you want to convert 0.444 into the fraction then first of all remove the decimal numbering from this original number. So, after removing the decimal number, the number will look like 444/1000 or 444 divided by 100. Simplify the fraction value. So, in the first step, divide the equation by 2. You will get 222/500. In the second step, divide it with 2 again. So that equation will be 111/250. So, the final decimal to fraction conversion of the number 0.444 will be 111/250.
