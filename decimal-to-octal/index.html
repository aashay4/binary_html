Decimal to octal conversion

The repeated division and reminder technique can help you to convert a decimal number into the octal. For example, if you are given a decimal number "8888" which you need to convert into the equivalent octal number. First, divide the number by 8. Divide the remaining Quotient by 8 again and keep on doing this process until you get 0. After the conversion, you will get this answer:21270.
